Sol a Solo

“SOL A SOLO” is a festival identity I designed to embody the poetic tension between solitude and connection. Through fragmented typography and organic textures, the visual language mirrors the event’s exploration of light, earth, and introspection, set against the lush backdrop of Lisbon’s Estufa Fria greenhouse. A minimalist yet evocative system that speaks to quiet gatherings and shared moments.
