Throttle Therapy

A motorcycle magazine that redefines riding as a form of active mindfulness. It examines the unique psychological state created when man and machine become one - where the body’s natural “power pose” posture lowers stress hormones while sharpening mental focus. The publication explores how this perfect balance of control and vulnerability, speed and stillness, transforms riding into a moving meditation. Through its minimalist design and immersive storytelling, the magazine captures the essence of motorcycling as both a physical experience and a therapeutic journey - where every twist of the throttle becomes an exercise in presence, awareness and personal transformation.
