Cartoon Network

This academic project involved creating a full branding package for a TV channel of our choice. I chose Cartoon Network and developed a series of animated assets, including bumpers, logo animations, and visual transitions. The work captures the channel’s playful and chaotic spirit through bold motion design, expressive character work, and vibrant colors—crafted to resonate with a young, energetic audience.
