Nº8

An editorial project exploring how endings transform into beginnings. Through a mirrored dual-book design and set of inspirational postcards, it reframes life’s closures as opportunities for rebirth—featuring stories of resilience from figures like Marilyn Monroe and Elon Musk. The number 8 (∞) becomes a visual metaphor for endless renewal, expressed through minimalist typography and raw materiality.
