Koenigsegg

During a visit to Circuito do Estoril, I unexpectedly encountered the Ghost Squadron, a group of Koenigsegg hypercar owners arriving for a private track event. Seizing the opportunity, with no prior setup, I focused on capturing the precision and artistry of their designs through static, detail-oriented compositions.
